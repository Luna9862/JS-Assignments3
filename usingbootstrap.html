About

Grow like a Rose was founded by an inspiration story. Many roses are cut and sold in stores. How many roses are thrown away if not bought. We believe that roses can bring happiness and appearance to one's homes. We also believe that due to inflation who would not want to save a couple of dollars by making your own rose garden. Grow like rose does not just grow Roses but all sorts of flowers.

Seasons

Grow like Rose is only offered during Spring and Summer Seasons. We have lessons for ages 15 and above.

Flowers

We are located in Iowa, a fun fact about Iowa is that Iowa's state flower is the Prairie Rose.

Own little Garden

In the beginning of the program each new gardener will plant any flower of their liking. By the end of the program each new gardener will have their own mini garden.
